## What this is

This is the official PAP device checklist to use before billing or requesting a RAD/PAP device.

## When to use this

- Before submitting the **first claim** on a new RAD device  
- Before **prior auth / pre-claim review** (if applicable)  
- When training new staff on required RAD documentation
