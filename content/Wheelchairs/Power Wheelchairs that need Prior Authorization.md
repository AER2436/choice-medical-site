# Power Wheelchair Prior Authorization Requirements

Need to know if a power wheelchair or accessory requires prior authorization? This quick reference guide breaks down what you need to get approved before delivery.

## Required Prior Authorization

CMS **requires** prior authorization for the following power wheelchair base codes before delivery and claim submission:

### Power Wheelchair Bases

- **K0800-K0802** (Group 1 standard)
- **K0806-K0808** (Group 1 heavy duty)
- **K0813-K0816** (Group 1 standard and heavy duty variants)
- **K0820-K0829** (Group 2 standard and heavy duty)
- **K0835-K0843** (Group 3 standard and heavy duty)
- **K0848-K0864** (Group 4 and Group 5)

### Seating Components

- **E2601-E2608** - Wheelchair back cushion systems
- **E2611-E2616** - Wheelchair seat cushion systems
- **E2620-E2625** - Wheelchair skin protection and positioning accessories

### Other Accessories

- **K0020** - Standard wheelchair cushion
- **K0195** - Elevating leg rests

## Important Reminders

> [!warning] Six-Month Validity
> Prior authorization decisions remain valid for **six months** after approval. If you don't deliver the equipment within six months, you'll need to submit a new prior authorization request.

> [!tip] Fastest Submission Method
> Submit prior authorization requests through the **myCGS DME Web Portal** for the quickest processing times.

## Additional Resources

For complete prior authorization instructions and to submit requests, visit the [CGS Medicare PMD Prior Authorization page](https://www.cgsmedicare.com/jc/pa/pmd.html).
